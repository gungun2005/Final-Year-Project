Crop Disease Encyclopedia

A guide to the common diseases detected by our AI system.

🍅 Tomato Early Blight

Identified by dark, concentric rings on older leaves. It often causes the leaves to turn yellow and drop prematurely.

Target: Solanum lycopersicum | Pathogen: Alternaria solani

🥔 Potato Late Blight

Water-soaked spots on leaves that turn dark brown. This is a highly aggressive disease that can destroy whole fields in days.

Target: Solanum tuberosum | Pathogen: Phytophthora infestans

🫑 Bell Pepper Bacterial Spot

Small, yellow-green spots that turn dark brown and irregularly shaped. It usually spreads via splashing rain or overhead irrigation.

Target: Capsicum annuum | Pathogen: Xanthomonas
